About HivePin

HivePin is a native mobile application designed to simplify real-time team collaboration and spatial note-keeping. Built for teams that move fast, HivePin allows organizers and members to anchor persistent data directly to real-world coordinates.

  • Team Hub Management: Organize, view, and decode real-time group allocations. Create team parameters and link team owners seamlessly.
  • Spatial Data Pinning: Securely anchor tasks, alerts, or details to distinct geo-locations for context-aware workflows.
  • Instant Team Sync: Experience zero-latency data updates when managing group rosters or regional objectives.

Google OAuth Data Transparency & Usage

HivePin utilizes Google Sign-In to provide a secure, frictionless identity verification process for our users. We respect your privacy and enforce absolute transparency regarding data access:

What Scopes We Request & Why:
  • .../auth/userinfo.email: Used uniquely to establish your login identity, tie your user profile to your specific organizational teams, and query relevant team ownership records.
  • .../auth/userinfo.profile: Used strictly to retrieve your display name and basic avatar thumbnail to cleanly identify you to fellow team members within your collaborative spaces.

Data Processing Compliance: HivePin does not request, access, or interact with sensitive or restricted user directories (such as Google Drive files, Calendar entries, or Gmail mailboxes). HivePin's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
